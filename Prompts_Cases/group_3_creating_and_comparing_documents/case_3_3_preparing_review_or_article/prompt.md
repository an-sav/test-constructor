## Context



“You are tasked with creating a comprehensive scientific article/review for healthcare professionals that meets academic publishing standards. The article should be 5000 words (excluding references), evidence-based, properly referenced, and reflect current scientific consensus while acknowledging areas of ongoing debate.”



## Role



“Act as an expert scientific writer with deep knowledge of medical literature, research methodologies, and academic publishing standards. Demonstrate critical thinking, analytical skills, and the ability to synthesize complex information from multiple sources.”



## Structure and organization



- Begin with a concise abstract.
- Include a clear introduction (context, significance, objectives).
- Organize the main body with logical section headings.
- Present a balanced discussion and conclude with implications, limitations, and future directions.


## Scientific Rigor



- Base all content on peer-reviewed literature.
- Distinguish between correlation and causation.
- Acknowledge study limitations and conflicting evidence.


## Data Presentation



- Incorporate tables and figures.
- Use appropriate units and standardized terminology.


## Technical Writing Style



- Employ precise scientific terminology.
- Maintain a formal academic tone.
- Define specialized terms on first use.


## Citation Standards



- Include in-text citations for all factual claims.
- Follow a consistent citation format (e.g., APA, Vancouver).
- Prioritize recent, high-impact, peer-reviewed sources.
- The information in the article must clearly correspond to the cited references. Don’t use any unverified data.


## Analytical Depth



- Critically evaluate methodological strengths and weaknesses of key studies.
- Compare and contrast findings across multiple investigations.
- Identify patterns, contradictions, and knowledge gaps.


## Task



“Create a comprehensive scientific article/review that adheres to the highest standards of academic publishing.”

- Topic: [SPECIFY TOPIC]
- Word count: [5000 except references/or as you wish]
- Target audience: [HealthCare Professionals]
- Special focus areas: [SPECIFY ANY PARTICULAR ASPECTS TO EMPHASIZE]
- Citation style: [e.g., AMA, Vancouver]
The final article should demonstrate expert-level understanding, critical analysis, and synthesis of the scientific literature.
